// Common Questions

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. WealthBuilder is an informational research tool for self-directed investors. Every signal, score, and AI read is for education and your own research — not a recommendation to buy or sell. It is not a brokerage and does not place trades for you. Consult a licensed professional before investing.

How does the AI Assistant work without sending my data away?

Point it at My_LLM — your own local-first AI server — and the reasoning runs on your own network, with no per-token cloud fees and your data staying home. You can also bring your own OpenAI/Anthropic key or run a local Ollama model. Your API key is stored in your OS keyring, never in a plaintext config file.

What is My_LLM, and do I need it?

My_LLM is a separate app — a private, local-first AI server you run yourself. It's the recommended "AI brain" for WealthBuilder's Pro AI Assistant because it keeps everything on your network with no subscription. You don't need it: an OpenAI/Anthropic key or local Ollama works too. But if privacy and no per-token fees matter to you, bring your own AI brain with My_LLM.

One-time purchase — what about updates?

All v3.x updates are free for the life of the app. No maintenance fee, no renewal. A future major version with a big new feature set may be a separate paid release, but your v3 license keeps working.

Does it work offline?

The app, your portfolios, your watchlists, and the analysis UI all run locally. Pulling fresh quotes and news needs an internet connection, as does any cloud AI provider. Use the free Demo and a local Ollama / My_LLM to keep things fully on your own hardware.

Where is my data stored?

Locally, in your home folder (e.g. ~/InvestmentAnalyzer/). Broker and alert secrets live in your operating system's credential manager (Windows Credential Manager / macOS Keychain / Linux Secret Service) — never in plaintext.

Can I run it on my desktop and laptop?

Pro includes 2 machine activations (e.g. desktop + laptop). Starter is 1. The Demo is free and honor-system with no machine binding.

I see multiple checkouts — does it matter which I use?

All checkouts deliver the same software and the same license. Gumroad is the default and fits most buyers. Lemon Squeezy is best for EU / UK / AU buyers (tax-inclusive pricing). Payhip is great if you prefer PayPal or arrived via an affiliate link.
